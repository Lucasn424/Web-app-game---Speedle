Game Info This game takes the base gameplay of wordle and incorperates time into it.

Guess the SPEEDLE in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. Check the colour blind slider and look at game info again to see new colours.

Grey signifies the letter is not in the word in any spot

Yellow signifies the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.

Green signifies the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
